Prepixo - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 December 2025

Prepixo ("we", "us", "our") provides this app and this Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as email address when you sign up or log in.
  • Usage data such as screens visited, actions in the app, and basic device information.
  • Crash and performance logs to improve stability.

How we use information

  • To provide core app features like authentication and saving your progress.
  • To analyse usage and improve features and performance.
  • To communicate important updates or changes related to the app.

Third-party services

The app may use services such as Supabase for backend and database services, and other similar tools for analytics or logging. These services may collect and process data as described in their own privacy policies.

Data retention and security

We store data only as long as necessary for the functioning of the app and take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it.

Children's privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us so that it can be removed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Data deletion

If you want to delete your account and associated data, email us from your registered email address at prepixo.official@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Prepixo account". We will delete your data within 7 days and confirm by email.

AI Services

This app uses Google's Gemini API to provide AI-based doubt-solving and learning assistance features. When you use the AI or doubt-solving sections of the app, your text input is securely sent to Google's servers for processing and the generated response is returned to you. No personal or sensitive information is permanently stored or used for training purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: prepixo.official@gmail.com.
